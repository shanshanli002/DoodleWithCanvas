BOB'S PRODUCTS

You might notice a familiar face in this application's UI, Bob Ross! Pixel Bob will be your host at Bob's secret desert where he needs help with his new products. You will be challenged to put on your creative cap to turn his products ready for the shelves.
This application was created for Mintbean’s hackathon and was inspired by Bob Ross' creativity and passion to inspire others. This application's mission is to inspire you to get creative and put a smile on your face!

Enter Desert

GUIDE

  1. Enter Bob’s secret desert and enjoy the view
  2. Read Bob’s welcome note to learn about your task
  3. Click “ENTER” button to view Bob’s latest product
  4. Create an awesome design by using your mouse: left clici, holding down, and moving
  5. Click “X” button on product pop-up to exit
